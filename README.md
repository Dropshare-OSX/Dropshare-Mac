## 🖌 Overview  

Dropshare Professional Suite streamlines the process of sharing files, screenshots, and recordings by connecting directly to your own cloud infrastructure and delivering a shareable link the moment the upload is complete.

The workflow is frictionless by design. Drop a file, capture a screenshot, or record your screen — and Dropshare handles the upload, link generation, and clipboard copy automatically while you move on to the next task.

Support for S3-compatible storage, SFTP, FTP, Cloudflare R2, and other providers means you are never locked into a specific service. Your files live on your infrastructure, under your terms.

Annotation tools let you mark up screenshots before sharing — highlight, draw, add text, and blur sensitive areas without opening a separate image editor.

Dropshare Professional Suite is the sharing companion trusted by professionals who need speed, reliability, and the confidence that their files are hosted exactly where they want them.
